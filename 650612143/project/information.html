Firefly is a playable character in Honkai: Star Rail. A member of the Stellaron Hunters and a young girl clad in a mechanical armor "SAM." Born as a weapon, she's afflicted with the agony of Entropy Loss Syndrome due to genetic modification. She joined the Stellaron Hunters in search of the meaning of life, relentlessly pursuing ways to defy fate.
